Koos Group posted:

Greetings. I'm going to be helping out with modding D&D and will be bringing a new moderation policy. Look for the following changes.

  • We will, generally speaking, no longer be moderating positions. This means you can for the most part advocate any idea you'd like. However....

  • We will more strictly be moderating the quality of arguments. Not whether they're logically sound, but whether they're interesting and in good faith. Which is to say, we'll be enforcing the D&D rules as written. So for example, if someone advocates a particular point, has it well-refuted, then comes back later to say the same thing acting like it didn't happen, that could be a probie.

  • The current incarnation of the US News thread will be moved to CCCC. This will be for the current group of regulars to chat about news in the manner they'd like. the_steve will, accordingly, no longer be a D&D IK. Then, there'll be a new, different thread for US current events, along with a recommendation to make individual threads for particular current events of great significance and potential for discussion.

This will also be posted in the D&D rules thread and USNews of course. I look forward to seeing you.
